About the Platform

Where Policy Intelligence Meets Precision Medicine

IPM is an independent, non-partisan initiative dedicated to monitoring, analysing, and explaining how personalised medicine is governed, financed, regulated, and implemented across countries and regions.

We operate at the intersection of health policy, science, regulation, and system governance — supporting evidence-based decision-making for policymakers, regulators, and clinicians worldwide.

View Our Reports
Our Role
To inform, contextualise, and clarify — not to advocate. We do not promote commercial, political, or institutional interests.
Global Coverage
Europe, UK, North America, Asia-Pacific, Africa, Middle East, and Latin America — 194 countries across the full care pathway.
What We Deliver
Structured briefs, decision monitors, comparative analyses, and thematic reports for policymakers, regulators, and clinicians.
Our Mission

Strengthening Personalised Medicine Systems Worldwide

By improving how policy knowledge is collected, interpreted, and shared — we make better decisions possible.

01
Transparency
Improve transparency around policy processes and decisions affecting personalised medicine access globally.
02
Reduce Asymmetry
Close information gaps between institutions and stakeholders navigating complex regulatory environments.
03
Cross-Country Learning
Encourage knowledge transfer between countries through structured comparative analysis and shared frameworks.
04
Equity & Sustainability
Promote sustainable, equitable, and evidence-based implementation of personalised medicine pathways globally.
What We Do

Systematic Tracking Across Every Policy Domain

IPM systematically tracks and analyses policy across the full personalised medicine lifecycle — from governance and regulation to access, workforce, and digital infrastructure.

We transform this information into structured briefs, decision monitors, comparative analyses, and thematic reports accessible to policymakers, regulators, researchers, clinicians, and civil society organisations.

Our platform integrates information from public institutions, official documents, consultations, expert forums, and peer-reviewed research to provide a coherent and continuously updated policy picture.

What We Do Not Do
Our boundaries define our integrity
Provide lobbying or advocacy services
Endorse political positions or commercial products
Offer legal or regulatory advice
Represent institutional interests
Replace national regulatory authorities
Promote or advertise commercial companies
We do not aim to influence policy outcomes directly. Our purpose is to support informed decision-making by improving the quality and accessibility of policy intelligence.
Methodology & Standards

Rigorous, Multi-Source Analytical Process

All published content is accurate, balanced, and contextually grounded through a systematic, multi-layered approach.

01
Continuous Monitoring
Official policy sources tracked in real time across 194 countries.
02
Source Validation
Cross-referencing, date verification, and contextual assessment of every document.
03
Expert Consultation
Advisory Council review and specialist technical input on complex policy areas.
04
Editorial Review
Peer review with strict editorial principles: accuracy, neutral language, transparent sourcing.
05
Longitudinal Tracking
Policy files updated continuously; methodological audits conducted annually.
