1. Cost Breakdown: On-Site Hiring vs Virtual Hiring

Let's compare actual employer costs for a mid-level administrative, technical, or support role.

A. On-Site Hiring (UAE + Saudi Arabia)

Hiring physically inside the country includes:

  • Monthly Salary (Market Rate)
    UAE: AED 4,000–12,000 (Skilled: AED 10,000–20,000)
    KSA: SAR 3,000–10,000 (Skilled: SAR 8,000–18,000)
  • Visa & Residency Costs
    UAE: AED 4,800–7,500
    KSA: SAR 2,500–6,000 (annual renewals)
  • Health Insurance (Mandatory)
    UAE: AED 1,500–5,500 annually
    KSA: SAR 1,000–4,000 annually
  • Gratuity/End of Service: 21-30 days salary per year
  • Office Space: AED 800–2,500/month per employee
  • Paid Leaves: 21–30 days + sick leave + holidays
  • Cost of Living Allowances: Housing, transport, phone

B. Virtual Hiring (Offshore)

60–80% cheaper through India, Philippines, LATAM:

  • Monthly Fee: USD 500–2,500 (AED 2,900–14,500)
  • No visa costs
  • No health insurance obligations
  • No office space (saves AED 800–2,500/month)
  • No gratuity liabilities
  • No mandatory paid leaves
  • Staffing partner handles recruitment & HR

2. Legal Compliance: Huge Difference

On-Site Hiring Requires:

  • UAE: MOHRE contracts, WPS, medical insurance, visa stamping, gratuity, Emiratisation
  • KSA: Iqama, CCHI insurance, WPS, Nitaqat Saudization quotas, termination compensation

Virtual Hiring Has ZERO:

  • No UAE/KSA labour law obligations
  • No visas or WPS
  • No Saudization/Emiratisation requirements
  • Employee follows their local laws only

Virtual workers deliver higher output due to:

  • No commute time

Proven roles: Admin, HR, EA, IT, finance, customer support, marketing
