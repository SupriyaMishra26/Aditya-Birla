Secure collection

We collect only the information needed to deliver orders, support accounts, and operate the site.

Purpose driven

Your data helps us fulfill requests, improve usability, and provide customer support.

Your control

You can ask us to correct, review, or delete your data, subject to legal retention rules.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, and contact number
  • Shipping and billing addresses
  • Payment details processed through secure gateways. We do not store full card information
  • Account and login information, where applicable
  • Website usage data such as IP address, browser type, and interaction history

2. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Fulfilling and managing orders
  • Communicating important updates about purchases
  • Improving the functionality and user experience of our website
  • Providing customer support
  • Conducting marketing and promotional campaigns with explicit consent
  • Meeting legal and compliance requirements

3. Cookies & Related Technologies

We use cookies, pixels, and analytics tools to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember preferences
  • Improve our site performance
  • Analyse visitor behaviour trends

You may control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may impact certain features of our site.

4. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard encryption, firewall protection, and access controls to safeguard your personal information. Although we take reasonable measures, no data transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Sharing

  • Payment processors for transaction fulfillment
  • Logistics partners for delivery
  • IT service providers for website maintenance

We do not sell or rent personal data to unrelated third parties.

6. User Rights

  • Access the personal data we store
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Withdraw consent for marketing
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to regulatory retention requirements

7. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we recommend reviewing their policies before providing information.

8. Policy Revisions

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically, and the revised version will be posted on our site with a new last updated date.

By using our website or services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
