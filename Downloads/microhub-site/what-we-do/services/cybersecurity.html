Rollout Logic

Security rollout should reduce exposure while keeping everyday operations usable.

We define where access should tighten, where visibility is missing, and how incidents should be handled before teams are expected to rely on the controls in production.

Control Identity and privilege boundaries are structured around real users, systems, and operational risk.
Visibility Teams gain clearer awareness of endpoints, unusual events, and policy gaps that need attention.
Readiness Response actions are documented in advance so incidents do not have to be solved from scratch.
