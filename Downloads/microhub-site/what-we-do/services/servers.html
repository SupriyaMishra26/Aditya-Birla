Rollout Logic

Server rollout works when capacity, recovery, and handover are planned together.

We baseline workloads first, then map compute, storage, backup, and support responsibilities so the environment is ready for production and not just installation.

Capacity Systems are sized around actual workload pressure instead of overbuying or running the estate too close to limits.
Recovery Backup and restore logic is defined early so the business knows how continuity is expected to work.
Governance Operational ownership, update paths, and support handover are documented before the environment is handed over.
