How does biometric sync work with payroll?

Face recognition attendance is automatically linked to payroll. Late comings, overtime, and leave balances are factored into salary calculations.

02

Can I configure EPF/ESI rates as per my organisation?

Yes, under Statutory Components, set percentage, employer/employee contribution limits, and applicable ceilings.

03

How are employee exits and full & final settlement handled?

Exit module handles resignation, asset clearance, leave encashment, and FnF settlement calculation with approval workflow.

04

Does it integrate with Zoho Books for accounting?

Absolutely. Zoho Payroll integration posts salary expenses and tax liabilities directly to Zoho Books, keeping P&L and balance sheet updated.

05

Can employees access payslips and request leave online?

Yes, employee self-service portal provides payslips, leave balance, document access, and request submissions.

06

Is multi-branch payroll supported?

Yes, manage employees across locations, different shift timings, and consolidated payroll reports.
