Everything about Ticketing Management

01

How does ticket creation work across channels?

Any incoming email, chat message, WhatsApp text, or social DM becomes a ticket automatically. Web forms and missed calls also generate tickets with rich context.

02

Can I automate ticket assignment?

Yes, set skill-based routing, round-robin, or workload-based assignment. AI can also detect urgency and route to senior agents.

03

Does Microhub offer SLA management?

Absolutely. Define first response time and resolution SLAs per priority. Automatic escalation and breach notifications included.

04

Can I import existing tickets from other systems?

Yes, import via CSV/JSON or use our API to migrate from Zendesk, Freshdesk, or any other platform seamlessly.

05

What kind of reports are available?

Live dashboards: ticket volume, FRT, ART, agent workload, CSAT trends, and custom reports with export options.

06

Is it secure and compliant?

Enterprise-grade security with role-based access, audit logs, PII masking, and GDPR compliance built-in.
