Rollout Logic

Rollout stays stable when software, hardware, and support share one plan.

Instead of splitting infrastructure into disconnected vendors, Microhub maps dependencies early, stages deployment logically, and keeps operational support attached after launch.

Scope Network, server, device, and support requirements are aligned before rollout begins.
Readiness Teams move into deployment with sequencing, handover notes, and responsibility lines already defined.
Continuity Support coverage stays connected after go-live so the operating model remains stable.
